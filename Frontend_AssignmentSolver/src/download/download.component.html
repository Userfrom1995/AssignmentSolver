Assignment Solver Instructions

Welcome to the Assignment Solver! Follow the steps below to upload your assignment and receive the solved document:

  1. Upload Your File: Click the Choose File button to upload your assignment file. Supported formats are PDF, JPEG, and PNG.
  2. Fill Out the Form: Provide your name, email, subject, academic level, and any additional information related to the assignment. This helps tailor the solution to your needs.
  3. Note on Additional Information: The Additional Information field is optional, but it's a powerful tool for customizing your solution. You can use this field to clarify any uncertainties, specify certain requirements, or provide extra context. However, be cautious—modifying this field can significantly alter the AI’s understanding and the final output. If you're unsure about what to add, it's best to leave it blank to avoid unintended changes.
  4. Submit: Once you've filled out all the necessary fields, click the Submit button to send your assignment for processing.
  5. Download Your Solution: After submission, your assignment will be processed. Once ready, the solution document will be available for download. You may need to refresh the page or wait a moment for it to appear.
  6. Manage Files: You can view, download, or delete your previously solved assignments using the options available on the page.

Please ensure that the file you upload is clear and legible to get the best results.
